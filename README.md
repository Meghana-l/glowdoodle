## ✨ Features

- 👆 **Air Drawing** — Draw in the air using just your index finger
- 🌟 **Neon Glow Effect** — Real glowing neon light tube effect
- 🎨 **Color Palette** — 5 neon colors: purple, red, blue, green, yellow
- 📏 **Thickness Slider** — Control brush size
- ↩️ **Undo** — Undo up to 10 strokes (Ctrl+Z)
- ▶️ **Start/Stop** — Full control over when to draw (Spacebar)
- 🗑️ **Clear** — Erase everything instantly
- 📷 **Mirror Webcam** — See yourself in real time

---

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Open the [live link](https://meghana-l.github.io/glowdoodle/) in Chrome
2. Allow camera access when prompted
3. Click **▶ Start** to begin
4. Point your **index finger** at the screen
5. Move your finger to draw glowing neon art!
6. Curl your finger to stop drawing
7. Click **⏸ Stop** when done
